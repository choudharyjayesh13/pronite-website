Why the monsoon is secretly the best time to visit Udaipur

Green hills, full lakes and low crowds — the case for a monsoon Udaipur getaway (with the parties to match).

Pronite Desk5 July 20262 min readUdaipur

Everyone visits Udaipur in winter, which is exactly why the monsoon might be the smartest time to come. The City of Lakes transforms in the rains — and for a getaway that pairs beauty with a great night out, it's an underrated window.

The city at its most beautiful

Monsoon fills Udaipur's lakes to the brim and turns the surrounding Aravalli hills a lush green. The palaces reflected in full lakes, the mist over the water, the fresh air after a shower — the city looks its absolute best, and the crowds and prices are lower than in peak season.

The party angle

While the peak outdoor season is winter, the monsoon still offers plenty — covered venues, rooftop sundowners with dramatic skies, and the run-up to our busy second-half calendar. It's a more relaxed, intimate version of the city's nightlife.

Make a weekend of it

Pair a lakeside stay with the city's food, heritage and views, and time it around a Pronite event if one lands. The Udaisarovar's villas-and-camps setup makes the perfect base. Come for the green season — you'll have the city almost to yourself.
