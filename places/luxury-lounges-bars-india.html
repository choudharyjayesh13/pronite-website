✦Expert advice from Pronite

Pronite's verdict: for a truly high-end night, skip the clubs and go for the sky bars. Mumbai leads — rooftop lounges atop its five-star towers (think Four Seasons' AER and the Taj's bars) deliver the skyline and the serious cocktails. Delhi's Aerocity and Gurugram hold their own; Bengaluru's high-rise lounges are excellent. Reserve a table, dress the part, and go early for sunset. These are about atmosphere and craft, not dancefloors.

At the very top end of Indian nightlife sit the luxury lounges and rooftop bars — the exclusive, design-led spaces atop the country's finest hotels and towers, where the emphasis is on skyline views, expertly crafted cocktails and a polished crowd rather than a packed dancefloor.

Mumbai sets the standard with its sky-high hotel bars overlooking the Arabian Sea and the city lights. Delhi-NCR and Bengaluru follow closely with their own high-rise lounges. For a special occasion, a sunset cocktail at one of these is hard to beat.

Why it's Pronite Verified

  • The most exclusive, best-crafted high-end nightlife in the country.
  • Spectacular skyline settings and serious cocktail programmes.
  • Reservation-led and polished — ideal for a special night out.
