About

Celebration, exploration, connection

Pronite began in December 2023 with one idea — that Udaipur's nights deserved more than the same old club scene. Since then we've produced festivals, NYE galas, Holi pool parties, techno open-airs and lakeside camping nights, working with national acts and the city's most-loved venues.

We strive to create a platform where every event becomes an opportunity for joy, every exploration unveils new possibilities, and every connection transforms into a lasting bond. That's why the next chapter of Pronite is a membership platform and app — one Pronite ID for tickets, perks and the people you party with.

Pronite is part of the JD Group family, alongside The Udaisarovar – Lakeside Paradise.
